# The Future of AI Interfaces

A quick thought on where interfaces are heading.

## From GUIs to Conversational Interfaces

Traditional GUIs require users to:
- Learn the interface
- Navigate complex menus
- Remember where features are located

AI-powered interfaces could:
- Understand natural language commands
- Anticipate user needs
- Adapt to individual workflows

## Implications for Knowledge Tools

Tools like Nalanda could evolve to:

1. **Auto-suggest connections**: "This relates to your note about [[Machine Learning]]"
2. **Summarize graphs**: "Your knowledge base has grown to include 50 concepts in AI"
3. **Answer queries**: "Show me all posts related to neural networks"

## Open Questions

- How do we maintain serendipitous discovery?
- What happens to the visual graph metaphor?
- How do we balance automation with user control?
